### 3. THE "DIASPORA LINE ARBITRAGE" PRODUCT

**The phenomenon:**
When Colombia plays a match, Colombian sportsbooks open different odds than European sportsbooks. Why?

Because Colombian bettors (in Colombia) bet heavily on Colombia → books shade the line against Colombia to balance their book.

But European books don't have this Colombia-biased flow → their line stays unbiased.

**The play:**
Your AI monitors the same match across books in multiple geographies. When a significant line discrepancy exists (driven by diaspora betting bias), that IS the value bet.

You're not predicting the match. You're exploiting the behavioral asymmetry between a local market and a neutral market.

**This has never been explained or packaged for non-English audiences.**

Product: "Diaspora Value Alert" — whenever there's a 5%+ implied probability gap between a local book and a sharp book, you alert subscribers.
