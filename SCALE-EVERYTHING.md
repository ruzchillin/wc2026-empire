### Multipliers

**5× affiliate revenue: CPA vs revenue share**
Most affiliates offer BOTH CPA (flat fee per signup) and revenue share (% of losses forever).
- At <100 signups/month: Take CPA ($60-120/signup, instant)
- At >1,000 signups/month: Negotiate revenue share (1-3% of all losses = recurring forever)
- WC 2026 signups who stay = 3-7 years of revenue share

- Same traffic. 4× revenue. Takes 1 week to apply and verify.
- At 1M pageviews/month: AdSense = £5,000 | Ezoic = £25,000
