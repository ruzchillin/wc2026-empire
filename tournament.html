Frequently Asked
Is this gambling?
No. The Oracle Race is a skill-based prediction competition, similar to fantasy sports or pub quizzes. Winning depends on your knowledge and prediction accuracy, not chance. This format is legal in most jurisdictions globally. Always verify local laws.
What happens if the AI wins?
The prize pool is still awarded to the top 10 human predictors. The AI competing is for entertainment and to prove the system's accuracy. An extra $1,000 bonus is given to any human who outscores the AI.
How do I submit my predictions?
After paying, you receive a personal dashboard link by email. Before each match (up to kickoff), log in and submit your prediction. Late submissions are locked out.
How is the prize paid out?
Prize payments are made via Stripe bank transfer within 7 days of the WC final. The full $100K prize pool is held in Stripe escrow throughout the tournament — neither party can access it until results are final.
What if I miss a prediction?
Missed predictions count as incorrect. The system auto-submits "Draw" for any prediction not submitted by kickoff. We recommend submitting all predictions for the group stage in one session.
Can I enter more than once?
One entry per person. Multiple entries from the same person are disqualified. Use your real name and email.
