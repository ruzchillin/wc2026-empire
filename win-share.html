The Model

How the AI Makes Picks

No black boxes. Here's exactly what goes into every prediction.

🔁
10,000 Simulations
Every match is simulated 10,000 times using Poisson goal distribution, team form (last 6), squad depth, and historical head-to-head data.
📊
Expected Goals (xG)
Model uses xG and xGA from qualifying campaigns — not just raw results. A team that "won ugly" gets appropriately penalised.
💰
Value Detection
A pick only goes live when our model probability exceeds the implied probability in the odds by at least 5%. No coin flips — only edge plays.
🌡️
Situational Factors
Group stage pressure, must-win games, squad rotation, venue heat, travel distance, altitude — all weighted in the simulation engine.
