## YOUTUBE SUPER CHAT STRATEGY

YouTube Super Chats are even higher value than TikTok gifts because:
- Higher income users on YouTube
- Messages are highlighted in chat (social proof drives more purchases)
- No age restriction for hosting
- Super Thanks ($2–$50 per video, works on VOD too)

**The playbook:**
1. Stream match reactions on YouTube in the 3 highest-CPM languages: English, German, Korean
2. Read every Super Chat message aloud (makes the buyer feel seen = more Super Chats)
3. Add pinned comment with Telegram link + affiliate link
4. After stream: enable "Super Thanks" on the VOD
5. Upload highlight clips as separate videos → each earns ad revenue

**Revenue benchmark:** GoodTimesWithScar (Minecraft) earned $80K in a single YouTube Live. Football during WC = same gift spike potential.
