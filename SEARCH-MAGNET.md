## THE CORE PRINCIPLE

Every Google search is an intent signal. Intent signals have value.
The job is: build the page that ranks for that intent, then extract maximum value from that visitor before they leave.

**Visitor lifecycle (every page, every time):**
1. Lands → push notification ask (PWA, one-time)
2. Reads → inline email capture after paragraph 2
3. Clicks link → affiliate revenue
4. Scrolls to bottom → Telegram CTA + premium offer
5. Moves to leave → exit intent popup ("get tonight's AI pick free")
6. Leaves anyway → retargeting pixel fires → they see our ad for 30 days
7. Returns → higher conversion because they already trust us

Every single visitor is worth $0.50-$8.00 in lifetime value if the squeeze is set up properly. Without the squeeze, they're worth $0.003 (one ad impression).

## SECTION 2: EVERY SQUEEZE POINT — IN ORDER OF APPEARANCE

### The 10-layer squeeze per visitor:

**Layer 1 — The Push Notification Ask (0 seconds after landing)**
- PWA install prompt + notification permission
- "Get goal alerts on your phone — free"
- Conversion: 15-25% of mobile visitors
- Revenue: Direct channel to 100K+ phones. Worth $3-8 per subscriber for retargeting alone.

**Layer 2 — Above-the-fold display ad**
- Google AdSense or direct sponsorship banner
- Placement: immediately visible without scrolling
- CPM: $3-15 depending on geo and content
- 10,000 daily visitors × $0.006 avg = $60/day just from banner ads

**Layer 3 — Inline contextual affiliate (paragraph 2-3)**
- Match preview page: "Bet on this match with bet365 — get £20 free" [affiliate link]
- Watch guide: "Stream it free with FuboTV — 7 day trial" [affiliate link]
- Team page: "Get [Team] official jersey — ships worldwide" [Printful link]
- Conversion: 1-3% of readers click. $20-80/conversion.

**Layer 4 — Email capture (mid-page)**
- Trigger: after reading 40-60% of content (scroll depth)
- Offer SPECIFIC to page type:
  - Match page: "Get our AI prediction for this match emailed to you"
  - Team page: "Get [Brazil] match alerts + lineup news"
  - Watch guide: "Get free streaming alerts when the match starts"
  - Standings page: "Get instant email when group standings change"
- Conversion: 3-8% of scrollers
- LTV: $5-15 per email subscriber over 38 days

**Layer 5 — Mid-content native ad**
- Second display ad unit mid-article
- Video ad if available (3× CPM of display)
- Sponsored content opportunity: "Analysis sponsored by bet365"

**Layer 6 — Telegram CTA (end of content)**
- "Get live match updates on Telegram — 47,000 subscribers"
- Social proof number increases conversion
- Converts 5-15% of bottom-of-page readers
- Revenue: 10% become premium ($19.99/month)

**Layer 7 — Premium upsell wall**
- After reading free content: "Want the sharp money data behind this prediction?"
- Soft paywall: show preview of premium signal, blur the rest
- Converts 2-5% of engaged readers
- Revenue: $19.99/month. 500 subscribers = $9,995/month.

**Layer 8 — Social share buttons (throughout)**
- Pre-filled tweet: "WC 2026: Brazil vs France prediction — AI says 68% Brazil. Who do you think wins? [link]"
- WhatsApp share: deep links to WhatsApp chatbot
- Every share = free traffic. 10% of sharers bring 2-3 new visitors.

**Layer 9 — Exit intent popup**
- Triggers when cursor moves toward browser bar (desktop) or back-swipe detected (mobile)
- Offer: "Before you go — get tonight's AI match prediction. Free."
- Single email field. One click.
- Converts 5-12% of people about to leave
- Without this: they're gone forever. With this: they're in your list.

**Layer 10 — Retargeting pixel (silent, on every page)**
- Facebook Pixel: fires on every pageview, every button click
- Google Tag Manager: fires for Google Display Network
- Twitter Pixel: fires for Twitter retargeting
- These visitors now see your ads for 30 days at $0.01-0.05 per impression
- Converts at 3-5× better than cold traffic when they see the premium offer
