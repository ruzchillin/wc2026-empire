The 5 rules of winning SGPs

What separates profitable SGP bettors from recreational ones. These rules come from 10K+ ticket analysis.

01
3–5 legs is the sweet spot
Below 3 legs: no payout boost worth building a parlay. Above 5 legs: correlation becomes too hard to track. 4-leg SGPs hit at the right rate.
02
Correlate your legs
If a team wins big, they likely had corners + cards. "Win + over corners" is correlated. "Win + opponent scores" is anti-correlated. DraftKings allows both — use this.
03
Include one -EV leg at long odds
Adding a +300 player scorer to a 3-leg SGP more than doubles the payout. Sacrificing slight EV for major payout upside is rational SGP construction.
04
Use xG not form
Form is priced in. xG isn't — not fully. A team with 2.4 xGA in their last 4 will concede eventually. Build BTTS picks around xG, not "they've scored in 6 straight."
05
Know which books allow SGP+ boosts
DraftKings: SGP+ boosts up to 35% on selected matches. FanDuel: Same-Game Parlay boosts up to 40%. These boosts literally change the EV — chase them.
06
Never trust "pre-built" lines
When a book markets an SGP in the app, they've already priced out most of the edge. Build your own from scratch using the parlay builder for better value.
