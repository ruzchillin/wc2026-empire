❓ FAQ

Are resale WC2026 tickets safe?

Yes, when bought from reputable platforms listed above. StubHub, Viagogo and SeatGeek all offer 100% money-back guarantees if tickets are invalid or don't arrive. Never buy from social media or unofficial sites.

Can I get tickets now for a match I'm not sure I'll attend?

Yes — most resellers allow free cancellation or you can relist tickets you don't use. Group stage tickets for big nations (Brazil, England, Argentina) will only increase in value closer to the tournament.

What's the cheapest way to attend WC2026?

1. Target group stage matches featuring smaller nations (cheaper demand). 2. Use Gametime 48h before kickoff for last-minute deals. 3. Kansas City, Houston and Monterrey have the lowest combined ticket+hotel prices. 4. Attend fan zones (free) for knockouts with screen broadcasts.

When do ticket prices drop?

Typically 48–72 hours before kickoff if tickets haven't sold. Also, prices drop significantly for games involving teams that are already eliminated or in last place. The best time to buy is right when the group stage lineup becomes clear.
