How to Buy Safely
✅

Always use platforms with buyer guarantees

StubHub, Viagogo, and TicketNetwork all offer 100% buyer guarantees — if the ticket is invalid, you get a replacement or refund. Never buy from Facebook Marketplace or individuals.

📱

Mobile delivery is safest

Prefer mobile tickets (QR code to your phone) over paper printouts. Mobile delivery means the seller can't duplicate and sell the same ticket twice.

⚠️

FIFA official resale is NOT widely available

FIFA's official ticket exchange is closed or very limited for most matches. The resale platforms above are the legitimate secondary market where fans actually buy.

💳

Pay by card, not bank transfer

Credit card payments are protected by chargeback rights. Never transfer money directly for tickets — no legitimate platform asks for this.

📅

Buy group stage tickets first

You know the dates and venues immediately. Wait for knockout tickets until after group stage ends (July 2-4) when venues are confirmed — then buy fast.

🚨

Scam warning: social media sellers

Instagram, TikTok, and Reddit sellers offering face-value or below-market tickets are almost always scams. Only buy from established platforms with buyer protection.
