Essential Travel Tips
🔥

Book NOW — hotels are selling out

Host city hotels are already at 60-80% capacity for tournament dates. Every week you wait = fewer choices and higher prices. Book refundable rooms immediately.

🌡️

US summer heat is serious

Dallas and Miami in June-July can hit 38-40°C. Drink water constantly. Sunscreen. Light clothing. Arrive at stadium shaded areas early. Know the symptoms of heat exhaustion.

⛰️

Mexico City altitude warning

At 2,240m above sea level, you'll feel breathless. Give yourself 48 hours to acclimatise before a match. Avoid alcohol the first day. Drink extra water.

✈️

Book flexible flights for knockout rounds

You don't know which city your team plays in for R16/QF until group stage ends. Book flexible-date flights or wait until group draw is confirmed for knockout venue.

🚌

Public transport beats driving

Stadium parking is expensive and chaotic. Atlanta (MARTA), Dallas (DART), and Seattle (Link) all have direct rail to stadiums. Check local transit apps before renting a car.

💳

USD is accepted everywhere

Mexican pesos and Canadian dollars differ, but USD is widely accepted in both countries for tourist areas near stadiums. Still, keep local currency for market food and tips.
