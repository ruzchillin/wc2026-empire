🏆 The Small Nation That Keeps Defying Odds

Croatia — The World Cup's Greatest Overachievers

Population: 4 million. World Cup record: 2018 runners-up (lost to France), 2022 third place (beat Brazil on penalties). By any metric, Croatia should not be able to compete with footballing superpowers — but they do, year after year.

The secret? A conveyor belt of technically brilliant midfielders (Šuker, Boban, Modrić, Kovačić), defensive organisation under Zlatko Dalić, and a national mentality that refuses to accept being the underdog. Croatia fans travel in massive numbers for a small country — Balkan diaspora across Germany, Australia, USA, Canada.

At WC 2026, Modrić leads them one last time. He will be 40. He won the WC 2018 Golden Ball. He won 6 Champions Leagues at Real Madrid. One last dream.
