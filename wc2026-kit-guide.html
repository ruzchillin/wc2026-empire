💡 Kit Buying Tips

🔍 Authentic vs Replica

Authentic kits (£90+) use the same fabric pros wear. Replica/Stadium versions (£60-70) are slightly different material but look identical. Both are 100% official.

📏 Sizing Guide

Nike and Adidas WC kits run slim-fit. Order one size up if you prefer a relaxed fit. Check each brand's size chart before buying — UK/EU sizing differs.

🖨️ Player Names & Numbers

Adding official name printing costs £15-25 extra. Order early — popular player customisation (Messi 10, Mbappé 10) sells out fast before and during the tournament.

💰 Best Prices

Amazon often has better prices than official stores, especially for less popular teams. Watch for Prime Day deals. Avoid unofficial kits on eBay — they're often counterfeit.
