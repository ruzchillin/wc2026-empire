- **source:** which page they signed up from (match-preview, live-scores, quiz, etc.)
- **country:** detected via IP geolocation
- **engaged:** opened 3+ emails in first 7 days
- **clicker:** clicked any CTA
- **premium-intent:** clicked premium CTA but didn't subscribe

- **referred:** came via referral link
