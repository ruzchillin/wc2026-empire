What is arbitrage betting?

Different bookmakers set different odds. Occasionally, the odds across multiple bookmakers for all outcomes of a single match add up to less than 100% implied probability — meaning you can bet on every outcome and guarantee a profit no matter who wins.

Example: Bookie A offers 2.20 on Team 1 (45.5% implied). Bookie B offers 2.30 on Team 2 (43.5% implied). Combined: 89% → 11% arb margin. Bet $455 + $435 on a $1,000 bankroll = $110 guaranteed profit.

This is 100% legal. Bookmakers hate it and will limit accounts of known arbitrage bettors. Best practice: spread stakes across new accounts, use exchange hedging, and rotate bookmakers.
