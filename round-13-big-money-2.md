### The Matched Betting Automation Tool
Matched betting is risk-free profit by exploiting bookmaker free bets and bonuses. It's legal, it works, and it's completely unserved in non-English markets.

Your tool: lists every current bonus offer across all sportsbooks → shows the lay bet needed on Betfair → calculates guaranteed profit.

UK market: OddsMonkey charges £30/month, has 100,000+ subscribers = £3,000,000/month.
Your version: target Nigeria (new bonuses constantly), Kenya (SportPesa bonuses), India (dream11 bonuses), Australia (massive bonus market).

These markets have no matched betting guide. You build the first one.
