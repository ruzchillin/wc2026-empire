🌍 New to American Football? (World Cup Fans, Welcome.)
If you followed WC 2026 and want to understand NFL betting, here's everything you need in 2 minutes.
The Spread
Like a handicap in football. "KC -3" means KC must win by MORE than 3 points. "BAL +3" means Baltimore can lose by up to 2 and your bet still wins. Same concept as Asian handicap.
The Total (O/U)
Bet on combined points scored by both teams. "Over 48.5" wins if the combined score is 49+. Like betting on over 2.5 goals in football — but NFL scores are 17–35 typically.
The Moneyline
Just pick the winner. Shown as -120 (favourite, bet $120 to win $100) or +150 (underdog, bet $100 to win $150). No draws in NFL — there's overtime. Works like a Match Result in football.
Same-Game Parlay
Combine multiple bets from ONE game into one ticket for bigger odds. Example: KC wins + Mahomes 2+ TDs + Kelce 60+ yards = much higher payout. Like a 3-fold accumulator on the same match.
