🖥️ How VAR Works at WC2026

✅ Goals — Offside Check

Every goal is automatically checked for offside using semi-automated technology. Takes ~60 seconds average.

🟥 Red Card Review

The VAR can recommend the referee review possible red card offences not caught live. Final decision always with on-field referee.

⚽ Penalty Decisions

Potential penalty incidents in the box are reviewed. Handball decisions most controversial — clear and obvious error standard.

🎯 Mistaken Identity

If a yellow or red is given to the wrong player, VAR can correct the decision without showing the card again.
