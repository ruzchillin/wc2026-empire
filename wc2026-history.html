⭐ Greatest WC Moments

1986 — Mexico City
Maradona's "Hand of God" & "Goal of the Century"
Diego Maradona scored two of the most famous goals in history against England in the same match. First the controversial handball goal, then a sublime 60-metre solo dribble past five English players — voted the "Goal of the Century" by FIFA voters in 2002.
1970 — Azteca Stadium
Pelé & Brazil's 4-1 Final Masterclass
Brazil's 1970 team is widely considered the greatest football team ever assembled. Their 4-1 demolition of Italy in the final, with Pelé scoring and then setting up the 4th goal with a clever lay-off, is the gold standard of beautiful football.
1966 — Wembley
England Win the World Cup
England's only World Cup victory. Geoff Hurst's hat-trick in the final against West Germany, including the famous "did it cross the line?" third goal, remains the most celebrated moment in English football history. "They think it's all over... it is now!"
2022 — Qatar
Messi Finally Wins the World Cup
The greatest player of all time completed his legacy with the most dramatic World Cup final ever played. 3-3 after extra time against France, Messi converted in the penalty shootout. Kylian Mbappé scored a hat-trick in defeat — but Argentina won on penalties to give Messi his trophy.
