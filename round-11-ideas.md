## CATEGORY 1: SPECIFIC BETTING MARKETS (HUGE, UNEXPLORED)

Every betting market below is its own product. You've covered match winners. These are the other markets where billions are bet and zero AI prediction content exists in non-English languages.

**1. BTTS (Both Teams To Score) Predictor**
The most popular global betting market after match winner. "Will both teams score?" — yes/no.
Your AI: historical BTTS rates per team, form, defensive quality → probability score.
Output: "Tonight's BTTS pick: Brazil vs Mexico — YES (73% confidence)"
Free: one daily BTTS pick. Premium: all matches with reasoning.
Asian and African bettors love BTTS because of simple yes/no format.

**2. Over/Under 2.5 Goals Predictor**
Second most popular betting market globally. Your AI gives probability for O/U 2.5.
Same content format as BTTS. Same audience. Equally simple output.
Historical average WC match: 2.6 goals. But variance is enormous by team pairing.
Your AI's team-specific O/U model is genuinely more accurate than bookmaker generic lines.

**3. Asian Handicap Predictor**
Asian Handicap (AH) is the dominant betting format in China, Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia.
AH removes the draw outcome — massively popular in Southeast Asia.
Almost zero AI prediction content covers AH specifically in Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Chinese.
Build: a dedicated AH prediction sub-product for your SEA channels. First mover in massive market.

**4. First Goal Scorer Predictor**
First goal scorer markets offer 10:1 to 50:1 odds. Huge upside. Fans love them.
Your AI uses: player form, set piece taking rotation, penalties, historical patterns.
"AI first goal scorer picks: Mbappe (12/1, confidence 23%)" — the format itself is engaging.
Free: one pick per match. Premium: top 5 picks with probabilities.

**5. Correct Score Predictor**
Correct score markets offer 10:1 to 100:1 odds. The dream bet for fans.
Your AI runs Monte Carlo simulations to find the most probable scoreline.
Content: "AI's most likely scoreline: Brazil 2-1 Mexico. Correct score markets offer 9:1."
This content goes viral because it's bold and specific.

**6. Accumulator Builder**
Users pick 3–5 matches. Your AI scores each selection, flags value bets, warns about traps.
AI gives the accumulator an overall "quality score" and expected ROI.
Premium: AI builds the accumulator for you — the best 4-match combination with positive EV.
Accumulators are the most popular bet type in UK, Nigeria, Kenya. Huge market.

**7. Clean Sheet Probability**
"Will [team] keep a clean sheet?" — drives: both teams to score NO, correct score 1-0/2-0, etc.
Your AI rates every WC defense. Published daily.
Content: "Top 5 clean sheet picks for today's WC matches — AI rankings."

**8. Double Chance Predictor**
"Win or draw" — lower odds, safer bet. Popular with cautious bettors.
Your AI identifies matches where double chance represents genuine value vs the market.
Premium: "Today's best value double chance — AI finds the gap between true probability and bookmaker odds."

**9. Half-Time / Full-Time Predictor**
HT/FT markets: predict both the half-time and full-time result.
Your AI's second-half model + first-half tendencies → HT/FT predictions.
These markets have high odds (3:1 to 20:1) making them compelling to share.

**10. Draw No Bet**
"If it ends in a draw, your stake is returned." Popular risk-reduction bet.
Your AI identifies matches where DNB value exists — where the draw probability is underpriced.

**11. Prop Bet Predictor**
Binary props: "Will there be a red card?" "Will there be a penalty?" "Will there be a VAR review?"
These drive: engagement, shares, in-play betting.
Prop bet content is the most shareable match preview format because everyone has an opinion.
AI gives probability for each prop. People argue. Goes viral.

**12. WC Squares (Super Bowl Squares Adapted)**
Super Bowl Squares is the most popular office gambling game in the USA.
For WC: users claim squares on a 10×10 grid. Grid rows = possible score digits at half-time. Columns = full-time.
If the final score digit matches your square: you win.
Nobody has built this for WC. First to launch = viral in the USA market.
Your platform hosts it. Entry fee optional (for legal simplicity: free entry first, paid version later).
