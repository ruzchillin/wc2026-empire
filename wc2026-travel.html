💡 Fan Travel Tips

📅

Book EARLY — prices are surging

Hotels near WC2026 venues are already 3–5x normal prices for matchdays. The earlier you book, the better the rate. Non-refundable bookings save up to 40%.

🏠

Consider Airbnb for longer stays

For 4+ nights, Airbnb is often 40–60% cheaper than hotels near venues. Great for groups watching multiple matches in one city.

🚌

Stay in city centre, not near stadium

City centre hotels give you transport options. Stadium-adjacent means premium prices. Take public transport or rideshare on matchday.

🌎

Multi-city USA trip is possible

USA domestic flights are cheap. A 10-day trip could cover Dallas → Miami → NYC with different group stage matches in each city.

🛡️

Get travel insurance

Match cancellations, flight delays, lost tickets — WC2026 travel insurance covers all of it. Check Booking.com's packages which include cover.

📱

Get a local SIM or eSIM

US roaming charges are brutal for EU fans. Get an eSIM before you leave home — T-Mobile prepaid from $25 gives unlimited data across all USA cities.
