💡 Fantasy Tips

🎯 DraftKings Value Hunt

In DFS salary cap games, the key is finding $6,000–$7,000 midfielders who'll start and contribute. Less-owned players give you a tournament edge.

👥 Ownership % Strategy

For big money contests, avoid players over 50% owned. You can't beat the field by picking the same players. Look for differentials in 10–25% ownership range.

🏆 FPL Captain Choice

For season-long games, triple-captain your best player for the knockout rounds. Save wildcards and chips for the last 16 onwards when fixtures are more predictable.

⚽ Set Piece Takers

In WC2026 with 48 teams, set pieces are huge. Players who take corners AND free kicks get double scoring chances. Yamal, Messi, Trent Alexander-Arnold.
