## THE BROKEN MARKET PLAYS

### 1. THE COGNITIVE BIAS COACH FOR BETTORS

Every bettor loses money because of documented psychological biases — not bad luck.

- **Recency bias:** Overweighting last match. ("Brazil won 4-0 last week so they'll win again.")
- **Anchoring:** The opening odds line anchors all future perception. If it opens at 2.0, it feels "expensive" at 1.8 even if 1.8 is still value.
- **Loss chasing:** After losing, betting bigger to recover. This is mathematically destructive.
- **Outcome bias:** Judging the quality of a decision by the result, not by the logic at the time.

**Your product:**
A "Betting Bias Detector" — users log their bets (or connect their sportsbook account).
AI analyzes their betting history and identifies their specific bias fingerprint.

"You show strong recency bias (83rd percentile). Your bets placed within 24 hours of a big result are 31% less profitable than your average."

This is genuinely new. Betfair's exchange, Pinnacle, Bet365 — none of them help their users understand their own psychology. That's not their incentive.

**Your incentive is alignment:** better bettors stay subscribed longer.

**Revenue:** $19.99/month. The product gets more valuable the longer they use it (more data = better analysis).
**Ceiling:** 50,000 users × $19.99 = **$999,500/month**. Pure software, zero marginal cost.

### 21. THE "PREDICTION MARKET" — LEGAL WHERE SPORTSBOOKS AREN'T

**The regulatory arbitrage:**
Sportsbooks are illegal in: India (most states), Indonesia, China, large parts of Africa.
Prediction markets are in a legal grey zone — and often explicitly legal.

**The distinction:**
A sportsbook: you pay money on an outcome, the house takes a cut.
A prediction market: you buy shares in an outcome from other users. Like a stock market.

Kalshi (US), Polymarket (crypto), Augur — all built on this model.

**You build the football prediction market for markets where sportsbooks are blocked.**
Take 2-3% of every trade (like a stock exchange, not a bookmaker).

India alone: 1.4B people, cricket + football obsession, sportsbooks largely illegal.
The prediction market model bypasses this.

At $10M in monthly trading volume × 2.5% = **$250,000/month** — from a market where no competitor can operate.
