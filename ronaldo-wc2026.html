⏱ Ronaldo's World Cup Journey

Germany 2006 — Age 21
🥉 1 goal — 3rd Place (best ever)
Young Ronaldo helped Portugal finish 3rd. Scored 1 goal. Controversy in the R16 vs England — Rooney red card. Still their best World Cup result.
South Africa 2010 — Age 25
😔 1 goal — R16 exit vs Spain
Scored in the group stage. Lost 0-1 to eventual champions Spain in the R16. Cristiano's best years, Portugal underperformed.
Brazil 2014 — Age 29
😔 1 goal — Group stage exit
Portugal crashed out in the group stage. Ronaldo scored against Ghana. Finished third in their group. A low point.
Russia 2018 — Age 33
🌟 4 goals inc hat-trick — R16 exit
Hat-trick vs Spain in an incredible group stage game (3-3). 4 goals total. Lost on penalties to Uruguay. Left Portugal wishing for more.
Qatar 2022 — Age 37
😢 1 goal — QF exit vs Morocco
Scored in the group stage. Dropped to the bench by Santos. Portugal stunned by Morocco in the quarters. Ronaldo wept. Questioned his WC future.
USA/Canada/Mexico 2026 — Age 41
🔴 The Final Chapter — All or Nothing
Now at Al-Nassr, training relentlessly. Declared he would play as long as his body allows. Portugal have a strong supporting cast: Bruno Fernandes, Rafael Leão, Gonçalo Ramos. 16/1 is value if you believe.
