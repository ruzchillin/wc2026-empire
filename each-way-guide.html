Each Way Betting WC 2026

Each way betting is the smartest way to back a dark horse at a major tournament. Instead of needing Morocco to win the World Cup at 80/1, you get paid if they just reach the semi-final — at 16/1. Here's exactly how it works.

📖 What Is Each Way Betting?

The Two-Part Bet Explained

An each way (E/W) bet is actually two bets in one. The first part bets on your selection to WIN. The second part bets on your selection to PLACE (finish high enough in the standings). Each part uses your stated stake — so a "£10 each way" bet actually costs £20 total (£10 win + £10 place).

In football tournament betting, "place" is typically defined as reaching the Semi-Final (top 4 in the tournament). The place odds are usually 1/4 or 1/5 of the win odds.

Example: Morocco each way at 80/1 (1/5 odds a place):
Win part: £10 × 80/1 = £800 + £10 stake = £810 if Morocco win
Place part: £10 × (80 ÷ 5 = 16/1) = £160 + £10 stake = £170 if Morocco reach SF
Total if Morocco reach the Final: £810 + £170 = £980 from a £20 stake.
Total if Morocco reach the SF only: £0 + £170 = £170 from a £20 stake (still massive profit).
