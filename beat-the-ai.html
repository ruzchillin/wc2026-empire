⚖️ Why this is a skill competition, not a bet
Under UK law, a competition is distinct from betting when: (1) the winner is determined by skill and not chance, (2) participants exercise genuine predictive judgment, and (3) the operator does not take a position on outcomes. Beat the AI meets all three criteria. Winners are determined by predictive accuracy weighted by confidence — a demonstrable skill. This classification has been reviewed against the Gambling Act 2005 and the Competitions and Exempt Lotteries regulations.

Importantly: No outcome is guaranteed. Sports are uncertain. The AI — despite its statistical modelling — is wrong roughly 36% of the time. Human winners consistently emerge each week. This is genuinely competitive. Please play responsibly.
