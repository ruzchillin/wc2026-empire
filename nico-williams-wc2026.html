⚡ The Nico Williams Story

From Bilbao to Berlin — Euro 2024's Breakout Star

Nico Williams came into Euro 2024 relatively unknown outside Spain. He left it as one of the most wanted players in Europe. His combination with Lamine Yamal destroyed every team Spain faced: his pace, directness, and left-foot delivery were unstoppable. In the final vs England, he assisted Oyarzabal's winning goal with a cutback that summed up everything — composure, vision, execution under maximum pressure.

What makes Nico special beyond the talent is his loyalty. Despite offers from Barcelona, PSG, and multiple Premier League clubs — reportedly worth £50m+ — he stayed at Athletic Bilbao. In modern football, that's almost unheard of. His bond with the club and its unique Basque identity has made him a hero in San Mamés and across Spain.

At WC 2026, Nico and Yamal are 22 and 18 years old respectively. Spain's wide duo could remain the best in world football for a decade. Betting on Spain at 10/1 partly means betting on these two dismantling defences like they did in Germany in 2024.
